🌀 Block-Snooze 🌀

Stay focused with automated tab blocking & timed snoozing.

🌟 Introduction to Block-Snooze

🟠 Block-Snooze helps you reclaim focus by automatically hiding or closing distracting tabs on your schedule.

🟠 Whether you need a quick 10-minute break or a full-day pause, set custom timers in hours and minutes.

🟠 Domain-wide snooze ensures that once you snooze one tab, all other tabs from the same site will be blocked too.

🌟 Core Features

🟠 ⏱️ Timer-Based Blocking: Automatically close or hide tabs when your timer expires.

🟠 💤 Snooze & Resume: Temporarily dismiss tabs now and reopen them exactly when you choose.

🟠 ⏳ Scheduled Closure: Keep tabs open until a future time, then automatically close them.

🟠 🔔 Notifications: Receive a brief alert just before any snooze or close action occurs.

🚀 Getting Started

🟠 Install from the Chrome Web Store or load unpacked in Developer Mode.

🟠 Click the toolbar icon to open the popup and schedule your first snooze or close timer.

🟠 Visit the Dashboard to view, unsnooze, or cancel any active timers in one place.

💡 Best Practices

🟠 🎯 Batch your focus sessions: group similar tabs and snooze them together for maximum productivity.

🟠 📊 Monitor your timers regularly in the Dashboard to avoid any surprises when tabs reopen.

❌ Cancel unused timers promptly to keep your workflow clean and uncluttered.
